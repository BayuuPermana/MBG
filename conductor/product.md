# Initial Concept
GiziSync is a fullstack management platform for the "Makan Bergizi Gratis" (MBG) program, designed to bridge the gap between local Kitchen Operators and the National Nutrition Agency.

# Product Vision
To be the central nervous system of the MBG program, ensuring that every meal provided is backed by transparent data, accountable procurement, and streamlined reporting.

# Target Users
- **Kitchen Operators (Cabang):** Responsible for the day-to-day execution of the program, including reporting on meals served and managing local inventory.
- **National Nutrition Agency (BGN) Administrators:** High-level oversight users who monitor program performance, set regional commodity prices, and conduct audits.

# Core Functional Areas
- **Commodity Price Management:** A centralized system to track and update the prices of essential food items, ensuring fair procurement costs across different regions.
- **Daily Kitchen Reporting:** A real-time interface for kitchens to report their daily activities, providing immediate visibility into program operations.
- **Automated Auditing & Procurement:** Integration of procurement workflows with reporting to create a seamless, audit-ready trail of all program transactions.

# Key Success Metric: Transparency & Accountability
The success of GiziSync is defined by its ability to ensure that every rupiah allocated to the MBG program is tracked and justified, providing a clear and indisputable record of program impact and resource utilization.
